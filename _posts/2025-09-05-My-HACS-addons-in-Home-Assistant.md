## What is HACS and why use it?

**HACS (Home Assistant Community Store)** is a community-driven repository of integrations, Lovelace cards, themes, and add-ons for Home Assistant that extend your smart home system ‚Äî all free, maintained, and created by enthusiasts from around the world. With HACS you get access to hundreds of solutions you won‚Äôt find among the official Home Assistant integrations. Installing, updating, and browsing available components is extremely intuitive ‚Äî almost as simple as installing browser extensions.
